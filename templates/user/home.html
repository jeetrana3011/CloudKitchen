Cheeseburger

A cheeseburger is a sandwich that consists of a grilled or fried beef patty topped with cheese, typically cheddar or American, and served on a bun. It is a popular fast food item and can be customized with various toppings such as lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, ketchup, and mustard. It is often served with fries and a soft drink.

Dosa

Dosa is a savory crepe-like dish that originated in South India but is now popular across the country and beyond. It is made by fermenting a batter of rice and lentils, which is then spread thin and cooked on a griddle. Dosa can be served plain or with a variety of fillings and chutneys. It is often eaten for breakfast or as a snack.

Pani Puri

Pani puri is a popular street food in India that consists of small, crispy balls filled with spiced mashed potatoes, chickpeas, or sprouts, and served with a flavorful spiced water, or pani.Pani puri is a popular snack or appetizer and is enjoyed for its unique combination of flavors and textures.

Pizza

Pizza is a popular Italian dish that has become a global favorite. It is typically made from a dough base, which is topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings, such as meat, vegetables, and herbs.Pizza can be customized to suit different tastes and is often enjoyed as a fast food or casual meal.
